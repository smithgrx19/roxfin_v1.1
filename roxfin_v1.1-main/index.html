Welcome to Roxfin Property Finance

Roxfin stands as a leader in property finance, delivering tailored lending solutions across South Africa. Whether you're securing funds for a property purchase, renovation, or improvement — or an estate agent needing a commission advance — we ensure reliable, fast, and secure support. With deep market insight and a commitment to service excellence, Roxfin is your trusted partner in property finance.

What We Offer

Sellers requiring an advance on the proceeds of the sale of their properties, whilst a transfer is pending.
We lend up to 80% of the proceeds, to a maximum of R500 000.

Sellers requiring funds to pay municipal and levy accounts and clearances, costs of CoC’s or other property-related expenses.

Owners of unbonded properties, requiring short-term loans. The borrower must provide the property as security for the loan.

Estate agents requiring an advance on sales commission.

We provide loans to owners of property who require funds for renovations or improvements.

Purchasers requiring funds to pay transfer duty where funds are available upon transfer.
